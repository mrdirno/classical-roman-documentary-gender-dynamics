## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene from the perspective of a household slave observing the silent power struggle between Lucius and Livia.
- Compose a satirical poem that might be recited in Clodia's salon, commenting on the performance of traditional masculinity.
- Draft a private letter from Livia to a (fictional) sister, explaining her strategy for saving the family.
- Create a ledger entry in Clodia's records detailing her 'investment' in Lucius Vorenus and its expected social return.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive model of the Roman familia as a power network, where legal authority (patria potestas) and economic influence are separate, dynamic nodes.
- Develop a text analysis tool that identifies and visualizes the 'gaps' in dialogue where unspoken negotiations between characters occur.
